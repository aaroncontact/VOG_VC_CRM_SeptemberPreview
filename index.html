I’ve spent the morning musing ways to sum up the “September effect” that electrifies the fashion world right about now without turning to that often-used phrase coined by former Vogue staffer Candy Pratts Price in—you guessed it—every fashion editor’s favorite style doc, The September Issue… After [significantly more than] one caffeine fix, I can’t shake the feeling that, this September, the oh-so-familiar pull of the new has itself taken a novel turn. While the buzz of shopping fall’s hero pieces certainly hasn’t lost any of its allure, this September is equally about seeking out new experiences and uncovering a newness that we can all be part of.

 

Through the Vogue Club community, we get to embrace the new season together—to share new hopes for the month ahead and say yes to new opportunities that bring us joy.

 

We’re thrilled to share our fall events calendar with you: From the blockbuster return of Forces of Fashion to our exclusive New York Fashion Week style-and-beauty lineup—plus a very special virtual get-together with the legendary Hamish Bowles—our editors can’t wait to share their career know-how with you. Who knows? It could be the encouragement you need to nurture that secret talent, or inspire your next big life decision.

 

One thing’s for certain: September 2022 really is the start of something great!

Member Spotlight: Let’s Get to Know Doncel Brown

 

We love our Member Spotlight series as much as you do… Which is why, from now on, we’ll be celebrating you—our beloved Vogue Club community—every week on site. Stay tuned for more details!

 

In the spotlight today: Vogue Club member Doncel Brown shares the story behind his fashion brand, Generation Typo, and the signature accessory that elevates his sleek, all-black looks—plus: What’s top of his fashion to-do list…

 

Full name/pronouns: Doncel Brown (he/him)

 

Location: Washington, DC and Virginia

 

My signature look: All-black, with an accent of color—the more layering, the better. I love having a custom briefcase on me at all times as well!

 

Favorite designers: Rich Fresh, Virgil Abloh, Jerry Lorenzo, Rick Owens, Tom Ford, Demna Gvasalia, Kaws, Samuel Ross

 

On my fashion wish list: The Met Gala; Paris Fashion Week; the CDFA/Vogue Fashion Fund; host a solo show for my brand, Generation Typo, and enter the metaverse; collaborate with some of my favorite designers; meet Anna Wintour!

 

A fun fact (or two!) about you: I created my fashion brand a year and a half ago––it’s all about sparking dialogue across different generations. (I also like to add hidden meanings (Easter eggs) throughout all of my designs.) Prior to my career in fashion, I worked in the tech industry, which inspired the augmented-reality elements which are built into my brand.
